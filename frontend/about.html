Welcome to Preloved.

We are a small startup business where they welcome all types of people and all types of clothing. Our aim is bringing modern day op-shopping and clothes-swapping to the interconnected digital world, while reducing fast fashion.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

Reducing fast fashion in the world is our primary motive, we want to find clothes that have been preloved so that someone else can give them a second home. We value the world we live in, and want to preserve its perfection.

Communication

We love to see people connecting through fashion and our site. Find likeminded, trendy people who love clothing as much as you do. Love yourself, and love those around you too.

Self Expression

Clothing seems like something materialistic on the surface, but it is an intergral part of self expression. Learning to dress to be true to yourself is so important. Stand out and be unique.
